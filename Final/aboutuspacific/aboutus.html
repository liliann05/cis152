About Pacific Trails Resort

Pacific Trails Resort is proud to present the nature and beauty of taking a vacation near the Californian Coastline. A vacation on the Californian Coast made for nature lovers and people looking to unwind. Our main mission for the team at Pacific Trails Resort is to create an environment that allows our guests to have the best possible vacation here on the Northern Coast. We have ammenties, and activities to accomadate all types of guests who choose to book a stay with us.

  • Our focus on Leisure: Pacific Trails Resort staff is here to offer services for our guests to have a pleasent and relaxing stay here on resort grounds. We have camping grounds and luxury yurt structures on site to accomadate for our guests. These sites are cared for and montiored by staff year round to ensure the highest quality accomadations and flourishing natrual environment for guests staying at Pacifc Trails Resort.

  • Our focus on Nature: Pacific Trails Resort is located by the beautiful Californian Northern Coastline. It's important for staff and guests alike to enjoy the scenary with respect to the native flora and fauna that is present and nearby resort grounds.We offer activites that allow guests to enjoy the nature at both a comfortable and hands on level: bird watching, marked hiking trails, and kayaking. Pacfic Trails Resort is located near the a state park. Join us with local park rangers to enjoy and perserve the natural beauty of the Californian Northern Coastal Region!
