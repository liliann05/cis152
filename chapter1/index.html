My favorite beverage is Jasmine Green tea. It's fragrant scent is quite delightful. I enjoy this specific type of tea, as it has the taste of green tea with additional flavor that I can enjoy with or without added sugar. Depending on my mood or the weather, I drink the beverage either cold or hot. On the occasion I go out for boba tea, if there is a selection of iced tea, I'm most likely to choose Jasmine Green tea with popping boba or fruit jellies.
